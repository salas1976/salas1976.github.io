Scenarios

Scenario 1

The user of the site wants to know the forecast for the day, which includes an hour by hour view of the temperature, humidity, air quality, and major weather events.

Scenario 2

The user wants to know the weekly forecast which will give them the average temperature for each day of the week and major weather events.

Scenario 3

Some user is checking the site to see what will be a good week to take time off to travel across the state to see their parents.

Scenario 4

Someone checks the site so that they know if the upcoming week is going to be too hot for their dog to be outside all day..

Scenario 5

A local running race director checks the forecast to see what type of gear the race participants need to bring for their upcoming race..
