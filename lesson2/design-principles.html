Proximity

Sweet Street

Visit Sweet Street

This picture from the Sweet Street website is a great example of how dynamic the Rule of Thirds can be in not only elevating a photograph but also enhancing the website. Please note that the front cake, which has more contrast in its coloring, is in focus and placed to draw attention. As you gaze a moment more, the three cakes diagonally create a line that leads the eye across the photo.

Alignment

The Lumineers

Visit The Lumineers

The Lumineers are known for their throw-back and simple style of music. This same simplicity is also conveyed in their website. This example shows that often less is more and the proper use of white space can do more for your website than overcrowding it. A clean design is more attractive to the eye and less confusing to the brain.

Repetition

Mekanism

Visit Mekanism

Mekanism is an advertising agency known for their innovation and creativity, which can be seen from their website. This image is a wonderful example of how powerful the principle of contrast can draw attention to web design. Not only do the colors contrast but there is a nice distinction between the texture of the background and foreground. This is also an ideal example of a photograph using the rule of thirds.
